GST Cranes exposes public, read-only resources for:

- used-crane marketplace discovery;
- Crane Wiki company and fleet-record research;
- wanted-ad demand discovery;
- public model, country, city, company, listing, and wanted pages;
- agent-safe browser handoff for authenticated user actions.

Public reads do not require an API key. Account actions, listing publishing, wanted-ad
publishing, credit spending, and billing actions require the GST Cranes browser UI.

Wanted ads are buyer demand signals, not for-sale inventory. Cite wanted pages only as
evidence of buyer demand.
